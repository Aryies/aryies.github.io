Computer Gaming

Computer gaming has and still is a valuable asset to society for economic growth and entertainment purposes. There are many lingering issues that have affected gaming as a collective and made gaming look bad in the eyes of the public. This website will demonstrate various points of views, explore the issues and elaborate on the connotations that entail.

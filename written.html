Purpose

To explain the various issues which come with computer gaming and the views, which are presented by the general public and our group. To also show our progress and thinking into how this topic effects people in society today and possibly what solutions can be presented for this issue.

Protocols

Link: Only accessible through links provided by the developers. It will not be found by Google as it is not indexed by them so a random search will not lead to someone being able to access our website randomly.

Hardware

MacBook Air: Comes with all of the programs we needed preinstalled ready for use without any problems. Fairly easy to use and is portable so we were able to work on our website solution from anywhere even if we did not have any Internet.

Software

Adobe Dreamweaver: Easy to use for website creation and allows us the freedom of the creation of our website. Allows for the use of CSS in our website to make it more visually appealing and easy to use for the general public.

Adobe Photoshop: Used for the resizing of various tabs and images. Used also for the cropping of various images to make sure that they are able to fit on our page and look more visually appealing.

Github: Used it to share with other group members and easy use for collaboration on the website. Also allows for free hosting of our website as long as someone is able to supply the link to the website.

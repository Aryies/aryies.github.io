Ethical Issues

Hacking inside a game would not be considered completely illegal but can be considered to be ethically bad. Hacking inside the game means the individual is not playing fair with other members of the game and is therefore cheating to win at that game. Companies usually are not able to catch hackers or people who use methods, which are classified as cheating so it is up to the individual on the matter. The individual has to think to him or herself whether cheating and/or hacking a game to win is the right thing to do, and whether they should be doing it in the first place. Morally, it is not right, but individuals may not see the wrong in this, which may result in future problems.

Some individuals may start playing games that are not age appropriate for them. For example, a 16 year old may play a game that is R18+. This opens up more issues as it means that they are playing a game, which may have a lot of graphic violence, nudity, drug use and other adult themes. Ethically it is not right the individual to be playing such games if they are not of the right age. The age restrictions were put in place there to protect teens or children who are not at the right age yet from such adult themes.
